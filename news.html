POLICY UPDATE May 20, 2025

New Sports Development Policy 2025

The government has announced a comprehensive sports development policy focusing on grassroots development, infrastructure improvement, and athlete welfare. The policy includes increased funding for rural sports programs and establishment of 100 new sports academies across the country.

"This policy will transform India into a sporting superpower by 2030" - Sports Minister

TRAINING PROGRAM May 18, 2025

Elite Athlete Training Camp

A 6-month intensive training program has been launched for Olympic hopefuls. The camp will be conducted at the National Institute of Sports, Patiala, with international coaches and cutting-edge facilities.

ANALYSIS May 22, 2025

India's Olympic Prospects: A Detailed Analysis

As we approach the next Olympic cycle, India's medal prospects look brighter than ever. With strong performances in athletics, shooting, wrestling, and badminton, experts predict India could achieve its best-ever Olympic tally.

"India has the potential to win 15+ medals in the next Olympics if we maintain this trajectory" - Former Olympic Coach Pullela Gopichand

The key factors contributing to this optimism include improved training facilities, better sports science support, and increased government funding. However, challenges remain in developing depth across all sports disciplines.
